# Contributing to AI-ML-Roadmap-from-scratch

Thank you for considering contributing! 🙏 Your contributions help learners around the world access the best AI/ML resources.

---

## How You Can Contribute

You can help in several ways:

- 📝 **Add Resources**: Suggest new courses, tutorials, blogs, or YouTube videos.
- 🛠️ **Fix Errors**: Correct typos, broken links, or outdated content.
- 📚 **Improve Documentation**: Make README, module descriptions, or instructions clearer.
- 💡 **Add Projects or Examples**: Suggest new projects or practical exercises related to AI/ML.
- 🧩 **Enhance Structure**: Improve module organization or clarify learning paths.

---
